Our Mission



The purpose of Klean Kellogg is to​ identify, formulate, and advance​
solutions to sustainability issues​
in and around the Global Hub and the greater Kellogg Community.





Our Values



Impact

Knowledge

Communication

Klean Kellogg will pursue only those initiatives that would have a substantial impact on the sustainability of Kellogg and its community. Impact could be measured in multiple ways, including but not limited to dollars, CO2, education, or awareness.
Klean Kellogg seeks to build knowledge relating to sustainability issues within the greater Kellogg community, but especially those officially committed to Klean Kellogg.
The dissemination of information is and will be central to Klean Kellogg's mission, and therefore it is critically important to establish and nurture efficient and useful communication networks throughout the Kellogg student body and administration.
